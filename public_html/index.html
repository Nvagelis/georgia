who i am

I was born in 1987 in Trikala, a small town in the middle Greece, where I'm still living. It is sourrounded with mountains and a river is crossing the town, which is a very inspiring place for me. When I was studying architecture in Aristotle university of Thessaloniki, I started my internship in Sotiris Samalis Atelier, and I have been working there since my master. While I was working there I was taking over some furniture and graphic projects by myself. I took some educational trips abroad related to architecture and I stayed in Torino for six months during my erasmus period. My passion for Research and Project Culture combined with the relationship between the Art of Making and Creating, have led me to an open vision of the project, which is constantly evolving.I am trying to create a strong critical sense and to have my eyes wide open so as to allow me to see what is apparently impossible to see. I believe that managing a transversal reading of contexts that don't seem to be linked together, will allow me to design things in a better way.
